Business For Sale Details
Listed on Jul 24, 2025(Renewed on Aug 26, 2025)
This unique opportunity is for the acquisition of two proven and well branded senior care businesses in two of the most desirable markets in Texas- Carrollton (Dallas) and Woodlands (Houston). Key employees (Marketers and Recruiter) are centralized for both locations. Owner performs executive level oversight, strategy, and fiscal management of the companies.

The business model supports senior and disabled clients to maintain independence, dignity, and quality of life. It is a simple business model- cash based and highly scalable with recurring revenue. Caregivers provide personal care, homemaking, companionship, incidental transportation and other home care services in the client's home or facility of their choice. Payer sources are currently private pay, Atena insurance and Long-Term Care Insurance. Weekly payments via ACH promotes strong and expedient cash flow.

The home care industry presents exceptional growth potential with the U.S. market valued at $150 billion in 2022 and projected to grow at 7.9% CAGR through 2027. The global market is expanding at 8.4% CAGR, expected to reach $515 billion by 2027. An aging population drives consistent demand for quality home care services.
