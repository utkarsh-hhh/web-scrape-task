Listed on Jun 14, 2024(Renewed on Aug 26, 2025)
For $500K, this 2350 sqft small animal general wellness practice with its real estate sits on a large, picturesque, two-acre property with tremendous future growth potential.

Loyal, highly-educated clientele. Includes a reception, three exam rooms, an operating room, kennel for inpatients/boarding, radiology room, large treatment room with a wet table and exam table, doctor office, and technician office. Ample parking, great road visibility and equidistant to two major cities. Includes digital x-ray, portable digital dental x-ray, mobile dental machine, IDEXX in-house lab suite, ultrasound, Digatherm, and cold-laser.

Collections over $900K. Top sources of revenue being pharmacy, exams, and surgery. Has option to expand services in orthopedics, physical therapy, integrative medicine, and ultrasound. Provides service to 1,400 active clients and growing about 20 new patients per month. Property has been zoned for livestock. Can convert the practice to mixed or an equine facility. (NYV101)
